🎯 The CinemaScore Algorithm

How We Find the Best Content

Our proprietary algorithm combines critical acclaim with audience love to surface only the highest quality movies and TV shows from the last 12 months.

Quality Score =
(Critics Score × 0.65) + (Audience Score × 0.35)
+ Bonus Modifiers:
• Certified Fresh: +4 points
• Verified Hot: +2 points
• Score Consistency: +3 points
• Large Gap Penalty: -3 points (if audience exceeds critics by 30+)

Minimum Thresholds:
• Critics: 75%+ on Rotten Tomatoes
• Audience: 7.0+ on IMDB or 70%+ RT
• Released within last 12 months

Why This Formula?

65% Critic Weight: Professional critics evaluate technical merit, artistic achievement, and storytelling quality with trained expertise.

35% Audience Weight: General viewers provide important perspective on entertainment value and accessibility.

Gap Penalty: Films where audience scores vastly exceed critical scores often indicate populist appeal without artistic merit, receiving a small penalty.

Bonus System: Special achievements like "Certified Fresh" status indicate exceptional quality consensus, earning extra points.
